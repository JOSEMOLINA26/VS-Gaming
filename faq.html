Frequently Asked Questions



What are your hours?

We are open from 2:00pm to 12:00am on Sunday - Thursday and 2:00pm - 3:00am on Friday and Saturday.


Is smoking allowed?

We do not allow smoking and vaping.


Is alcohol sold here?

We do not sell alcohol.


Is drinking alcohol allowed?

We do not allow people to drink alcohol inside the store.


Do you guys serve food?

We serve a variety of snacks and candy that can be purchased at the front.


Can I host an event/birthday party here?

We haven't officially started hosting parties, please inquire about events in person.
